Kyle Addison Tynes

User Experience / User Interface (UX/UI) Designer & Researcher.

Specializes in user research and wireframing/prototyping.

Kyle has been creative since childhood. He has been performing arts ever since he can remember. From drawing to playing instruments to digital art and design. Kyle always tries to see from others' perspectives. His compassion and empathy for others has built a strong quality for problem solving. Kyle has interest in many branches of UX/UI design and seeks to learn more about them. Kyle’s heart and passion resides in video games and hopes to achieve a career in the gaming industry. He knows he would enjoy designing HUDs (heads-up display), menus, ect. but feels that games might not be getting enough user testing and feedback before their release date. VR (virtual reality) is also growing in popularity with some users experiencing difficulties. Kyle believes that with his knowledge and experience in user research and empathy, he can help.

MY UX SKILLS

User Research

User research focuses on understanding user behaviors, needs, and motivations through observation techniques, task analysis, and other feedback methodologies.

User Interface Design

User interface (UI) design is the process designers use to build interfaces in software or computerized devices, focusing on looks or style.

Web Development

The web development process includes web design, web content development, client-side/server-side scripting and network security configuration, among other tasks.
